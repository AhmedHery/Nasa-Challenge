LANDSAT

  • Status : Current, Extended Mission.
  • Mission Category : Earth Observing System (EOS).
  • Launch Date : April 15, 1999.
  • Launch Location : Vandenberg Air Force Base, CA.
  • Relevant Science Focus Areas :
  • Carbon Cycle, Ecosystems, and Biogeochemistry.
  • Earth Surface and Interior.

Science Goals

  • Acquire sunlit, essentially cloud-free global seasonal coverage of Earth & rsquo;s land masses.
  • Provide well-calibrated radiometric and geometric data.
  • Meet requirements for global-change research by providing imagery that are consistent
    with the acquisition geometry, spatial resolution, spectral.
  • characteristics, and calibration of previous Landsat data.

Related Applications

  • Agricultural Efficiency.
  • Air Quality.
  • Aviation.
  • Carbon Management.
  • Coastal Management.
  • Disaster Management.
  • Ecological Forecasting.
  • Energy Management.
  • Homeland Security.
  • Invasive Species.
  • Public Health.
  • Water Management.
